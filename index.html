15. THE OUTER MEASURE GENERATED BY A MEASURE

Throughout this section, (X,S,μ)(X, \mathcal{S}, \mu)(X,S,μ) will be a fixed measure space. Our main objective here is to study the remarkable properties of the outer measure μ∗\mu^*μ∗ generated by μ\muμ.
